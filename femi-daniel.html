Kenny Noah

With over 17-year of experience in the information technology sector, Kenny Noah has established himself as a distinguished professional in the realms of Cloud and cybersecurity. Presently, Kenny is a Technical Account Manager at Google, where his expertise is instrumental in guiding clients through the design, implementation, and successful integration of cloud solutions.

In his role at Google, Kenny Noah collaborates closely with clients to comprehend their unique requirements and challenges in the operationalization and adoption of diverse cloud solutions. His duties encompass facilitating the seamless migration and optimization of workloads in the cloud, ultimately ensuring that organizations meet their business goals through technology.

Kenny Noah's career path has spanned prominent organizations such as VMware and Hewlett Packard Enterprise, where he contributed significantly to mission-critical migration projects and played a pivotal role in guiding organizations through technology adoption and digital transformation endeavors.

Kenny also leads the TAM Application Modernization Subject Matter Expert Community at Google Cloud, where he plays a central role in the enhancement of software supply-chain security through the Supply-chain Level for Software Artifacts (SLSA) Assessment initiative. SLSA is a security framework designed to thwart tampering, enhance integrity, and secure software packages.

Outside of his professional endeavors, Kenny is driven by a passionate commitment to mentoring and empowering underrepresented individuals and young aspiring technologists, guiding them in the development of crucial technology skills.
