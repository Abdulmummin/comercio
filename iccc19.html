The First Edition

The First Edition of the ICCC was held in 2019

The first edition of the conference was held in 2019 at Four Points by Sheraton, Victoria Island, Lagos, in collaboration with Interswitch.

The very first edition of the International Cybersecurity and Cloud Conference by Comercio Limited themed The Business of Cybersecurity and Cloud in 21st Century Nigeria held on 4th October, 2019. Hosted at Four Points by Sheraton, this monumental event saw speakers like Taiye Lambo of eFortresses CloudeAssurance, Rakiya Mohammed of the Central Bank of Nigeria, and Mitchell Elegbe, the founder of Interswitch and more seasoned individuals in the business of cybersecurity and cloud. The 21st century has witnessed a remarkable digital transformation across various sectors in Africa, leading to an increased reliance on technology, data, and interconnected systems. With this rapid technological growth, the demand for robust cybersecurity and efficient cloud services has grown exponentially. Against this backdrop, the conference highlighted the opportunities in the industry, the challenges and strengths and overall, the need for collaborative efforts was emphasised in order for the industry to thrive.
