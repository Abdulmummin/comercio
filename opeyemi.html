Below is a short biography of Opeyemi Onifade

Opeyemi Onifade

Opeyemi stewards the proficiencies of astute Information Technology and Cybersecurity professionals at Afenoid Enterprise Limited which he founded in 2011 after leaving his role as Chief Information Security Officer of a large enterprise.

He has led Afenoid Enterprise Limited to become a leading Payment Card Industry Qualified Security Assessment (PCIQSA) Company in the region, SWIFT Cybersecurity Solutions Provider and Digital Trust accredited training organization.

He is regarded as a cybersecurity and digital transformation coach, trusted advisor and strategic partner by a number of public and private sector executives across up to 40 countries in Africa, Middle-East and Asia.

He currently serves the professional community as the Africa Ambassador for ISACA Global and Board Member of Africa ICT Alliance, AFICTA.
