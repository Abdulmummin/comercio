Below is a short biography of Dr Maryleen Amaizu

Maryleen Amaizu - Data & AI Architect

Maryleen is an AI researcher and Architect who uses her experience in data science, machine learning, and cloud computing to design and implement cutting-edge data infrastructure and AI solutions for organizations. She was a finalist for the Young CISO of the Year award and winner of the Excellence Award in Data Protection and Information Privacy.

She is also a recipient of the UK and Australia Global Talent Visa for her leadership and achievements in the digital technology sector. She is the visioneer of Scholarleen, a global talent mobility initiative, which connects talents to opportunities. She holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Leicester UK, where her research focused on AI deployment in IoT environments.
