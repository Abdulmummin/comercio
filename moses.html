Below is a short biography of Mr Moses Faya

Moses Faya

Moses is a seasoned Technology Attorney with extensive experience in commercial law, particularly in technology-related areas. He specializes in addressing legal, policy, and business challenges in the areas of digital economy, startups, data protection, Financial Technology, Regulation Technology, and other emerging technologies.

He founded Tech Policy Advisory, a firm dedicated to navigating the crossroads of law, business, and technology. Their mission is to deliver value by supporting both public and private sector clients in understanding and adapting to the evolving tech landscape.

Moses is deeply committed to digital transformation. He has been at the forefront of several initiatives, including membership in the Digital NBA and an executive role in the NBA-SBL ICT Committee. His leadership has been instrumental in building the capacity of commercial lawyers in Nigeria to tackle the legal complexities arising from the digital economy's growth.

Through these efforts, he has significantly influenced the evolution of technology law in Nigeria. Driven by a passion for knowledge dissemination and technological progress, Moses remains dedicated to shaping the future of technology law, policy, and governance in Nigeria and globally.
